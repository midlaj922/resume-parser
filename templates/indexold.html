SkillWise Recruit

Empowering Recruitment with Smart Resume Analysis

About This Project

SkillWise Recruit is an innovative tool designed to streamline the hiring process by analyzing resumes and matching candidates to suitable job roles. Leveraging advanced text extraction and skill-based algorithms, it identifies key skills and recommends career paths based on market trends.

  • Upload multiple PDFs and analyze them instantly.
  • Get personalized job recommendations based on skills.
  • Download results as a professional PDF report.
  • Handles up to 15 resumes with robust error management.
